The Challenge & Solution

Why AquaSync IoT?

Traditional irrigation is wasteful and manual. We built a smarter alternative.

🚨 The Problem

Manual irrigation in Indian agriculture causes massive water wastage. Farmers irrigate on fixed schedules — not based on actual soil needs — leading to over-watering, crop damage, and rising water bills.

No real-time soil data
Over-irrigation damages roots
Cannot monitor remotely
High manual labour costs

✅ Our Solution

AquaSync IoT automates the entire irrigation process using ESP32, soil moisture & DHT11 sensors, and the Blynk IoT cloud — enabling smart, data-driven irrigation from anywhere.

System Flow

How It Works

From sensor reading to pump activation — all automated in real time.

01

Soil Sensor Detects Moisture

The soil moisture sensor continuously monitors ground water levels. When moisture drops below the threshold, it triggers the system to act.

02

Data Sent to ESP32

The ESP32 microcontroller collects sensor readings from both the soil moisture sensor and the DHT11 (temperature & humidity) in real time.

03

Smart Decision Logic Runs

The embedded If/Then automation logic evaluates sensor data. If moisture is low, the system initiates irrigation. If adequate, it stays off.

04

Relay Controls Water Pump

A relay module switches the water pump ON/OFF based on the decision logic — ensuring precise, need-based water delivery to crops.

05

User Monitors via Mobile App

Live data is pushed to the Blynk IoT cloud. The farmer can monitor soil levels, temperature, humidity, and control the pump remotely from anywhere.

Advantages

Key Features

Why SmartIrriga stands out from traditional irrigation methods.

Water Saving

Smart threshold logic prevents over-irrigation — saving up to 40% of water compared to manual methods.

Full Automation

Automated If/Then rules eliminate human error and deliver water exactly when the soil needs it.

Remote Access

Monitor and control your irrigation system from any smartphone, anywhere in the world via Blynk cloud.

Precise irrigation based on real soil data prevents root rot and ensures maximum crop yield.
